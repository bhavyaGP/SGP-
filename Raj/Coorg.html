About

Welcome to the picturesque land of Coorg, nestled in the Western Ghats of Karnataka, India. Fondly known as the "Scotland of India," Coorg captivates travelers with its lush greenery, misty mountains, and serene landscapes. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of coffee plantations that stretch as far as the eye can see. Explore the charming hill station of Madikeri, with its historic sites and vibrant markets. Don't miss the mesmerizing Abbey Falls, where gushing waters cascade amidst verdant surroundings. For nature enthusiasts, a visit to Nagarhole National Park is a must, where you can witness diverse wildlife in their natural habitat. Savour the local cuisine and savor the region's signature coffee as you experience the warm hospitality of the Coorg people. Whether you seek adventure, tranquility, or cultural experiences, Coorg and its surrounding places offer an unforgettable journey of discovery.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Coorg is during the months of October to April when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities. During this time, the region is adorned with lush greenery, and the coffee plantations are at their scenic best. The monsoon season from June to September brings heavy rainfall and may not be suitable for outdoor adventures, but it transforms Coorg into a lush paradise. However, if you are a fan of waterfalls, the monsoon season is perfect for witnessing them in their full glory. Whether you seek a serene retreat amidst nature's embrace or wish to explore the rich cultural heritage, Coorg offers a memorable experience throughout the year, but the cooler months are the most popular among tourists.
